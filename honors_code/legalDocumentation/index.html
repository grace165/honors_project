Legal Documentation

This section is used to describe and list all legal and formal documentation needed in the startup of a small business.

Articles of Organization
Assigned once you have registered your Limited Liability Company (LLC) with the State Corporation Commission (SCC). You also receive a certificate for the creation of the LLC.

Tax FEIN
Needed to act as the business's SSN and invidiual identification for applying for a business license and filing your taxes.

Business License
Required by state or local government in order to do business in that locality. (not required in all locations)

Zoning Permit
Needed by local governments to show that you are allowed to do business where you want to. (Home Occupation Permits needed as well)
