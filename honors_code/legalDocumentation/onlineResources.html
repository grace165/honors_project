Online Resources

This page is used to list resources that you can access online in the creation of a small business.

Tutorials Page

Tutorials made by me for the purpose of helping new small business owners with the more complicated parts of the startup process.

(links to other helpful resources in descriptions of YouTube videos)


SCC Business Name Availability Check

This site is very useful for checking if your small business name ideas are already in use.


Requirements for Business Name

This site lists certain naming rules and conventions required for different business types.

(keep these conditions in mind when thinking of business names)


Truist Free Business Bank Account

Truist banking offers a free simple business checking account with basic functions. This is perfect for a business startup with less than 50 expected monthly transactions.


Wix free web development

Wix provides a free software for setting up your initial website to share your business with customers.

(great for first time small business owners looking to have a way to connect with their target market)


IRS Tax Information for Businesses

This site provides access to tax information for new businesses and how to file for business taxes with the federal government.


Virginia Department of Taxation

This site provides access to all tax information for Virginia businesses and how to file for business taxes such as sales taxes, employer withholdings etc.


Augusta County Complete Resource Guide

This page provides a list of all useful resources available in Augusta county to new small businesses.


Valley SBDC Resources

This site provides another list of resources on the state and federal level that will aid in the setup of your small business.
