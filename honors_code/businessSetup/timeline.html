Timeline

The timeline below contains each step taken and the date to show an accurate representation of how my honors project came to be.

April 2023: Discuss plans for project with potential project advisor and Honors Director.

⤷

Summer 2023: Selected committee, planned for project, and researched (target market, business idea viability, local graphics businesses etc.)

First week of Fall classes (Aug. 22nd–Aug.29th): Registered for BUS-499, Honors Application and signatures, and Honors Upgrade Form

⤶
⤷

August 30th–September 6th: Chose business name (Graphics Gremlin LLC) and structure–I chose the LLC because of the separation from personal liability.

September 7th: Contacted Greg Godsey (I want to credit him for connecting me with Allison Dugan at the JMU SBDC)

⤶
⤷

September 12th: Contacted SBDC–Allison Dugan for help in knowing where to start when opening a small business for the first time(she was extremely helpful throughout my entire project)

September 13th: Met with Derek Smiley, owner of Smiley's Ice Cream in Bridgewater, for insight on owning a small business

⤶
⤷

September 15th: Opened Azure Web Server (after some difficulties with Bridgewater College IT)

September 21st: First meeting with Allison Dugan (met over Zoom and discussed initial processes and steps for a business startup)

⤶
⤷

September 29th: First draft of website due (initial submission of my website entailed the beginning setup and wireframes planned out using HTML and CSS)

October 17th: Applied for funding with the Business Administration Department (and generously received $200 to fund my startup)

⤶
⤷

October 19th: Met with Rebekah Castle, Director of Economic Development and Marketing, at the Augusta Government Center (we discussed the necessary documents and filings for the localities I would be doing business in)

October 23rd: Filled out business plan and met with Allison Dugan (we walked through my plan and made changes to outline the details and future of my small business)

⤶
⤷

October 31st: Registered with SCC! (this signified the official start of my small business with the LLC Certificate and Articles of Organization being created)

November 1st: Registered with the IRS and received FEIN (for tax purposes and so that I could apply for a business license in Augusta County)

⤶
⤷

November 8th: Created Instagram and Facebook accounts and posted for the first time

November 9th: Applied and received business license and zoning permit (I ended up visiting the Government Center 3 days in a row to get this completed. It was a long process working with both Community Development and the Commissioner of Revenues Office...)

⤶
⤷

November 10th: Created Wix site (the Graphics Gremlin LLC website is a simple representation of what a small business website could look like)

November 14th: Recorded YouTube tutorials (this took a long time–my original video editing software decided to charge money for basic features, so I had to change my plans, quickly)

⤶
⤷

November 21st: Finished Website! (I spent a long time finishing the site because of the CSS. The site is not perfect, but I am happy with the flex implementations I was able to add. Try resizing the page and watch the boxes change in size!)

November 27th: Oral presentation
