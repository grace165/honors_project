Sources

Compilation of all notes, research, and interviews for insight

Sources used to create my list of steps:

“Ten Steps to Start Your Business” by the U.S. Small Business Administration

⤷

    Steps:
    1. Conduct market research
    2. Write business plan
    3. Find funding for business
    4. Pick location
    5. Decide business type and structure
    6. Choose business name
    7. Register business
    8. Get federal and state tax IDs
    9. Apply for licenses and permits
    10. Open a business bank account

“How to Start a Business: A Small Business Checklist” by WGU

⤷

    Steps:
    1. Begin with a solid business idea
    2. Market research / identify target market
    3. Develop a business plan
    4. Get a small business loan
    5. Hire employees
    6. Improvise and adapt
    7. Be patient

“Launch Your Business” by the U.S. Small Business Administration

⤷

    Steps:
    1. Pick business location
    2. Choose business structure
    3. Choose business name
    4. Register business as legal entity
    5. Get federal and state tax IDs
    6. Apply for licenses and permits
    7. Open business bank account
    8. Get business insurance

“Start a New Business” by the State Corporation Commission

⤷

    Steps:
    1. Select business type
    2. Choose business name
    3. Select registered agent
    4. Register with SCC

Sources for Graphic Design Research:

“How to Start a Graphic Design Business: 16 Do's and Don'ts from the Pros” by Rasmussen University

⤷

    Summary of Do's and Don'ts:
    1. Your work has value
    2. Don't underestimate strong portfolio power
    3. Craft business plan
    4. Don't expect instant success
    5. Find a mentor
    6. Don't do work just for exposure
    7. Be authentic
    8. Don't lost sight of your 'why'
    9. Trust your instincts
    10. Don't say yes to every project
    11. Find your specialty or niche
    12. Diversify clientelessssss
    13. Familiarize yourself with copywrights
    14. Don't do work without contract
    15. Do good/donate time for what you are passionate about
    16. Don't quit your day job
