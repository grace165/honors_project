How to Create A Small Business, Website, and New Career

Welcome to my Honors Project! In an effort to create an original project, I have chosen to make a small graphic design business and document my procedures in a website. This will entail the creation of both the business and the website in order to fully encompass my experiences at Bridgewater College. The focus will be on the small business and the necessary steps to open a business. Each step will be carefully documented, so that anyone interested in opening a business can follow the same procedures. The steps will be recorded in a “how-to“ style on the Azure website I create. This process will include the use of JavaScript frameworks. I will also use a combination of images, text, links, and videos (linked to my YouTube) to properly convey my message. The graphic design business created will ultimately serve as an example of how to set up a small business; moreover, the website will emphasize the guidelines I create by adding real examples from my experiences. For example, any fees paid or paperwork signed to open the business will be documented. I expect the website itself to have multiple pages and sections with personal stories and advice (without sounding too much like a blog). In the end, I am aiming to produce an honors project that will undeniably represent all of my interests – this project will serve as a great culmination of my college education.

My plan is to begin with a simple question: How does one open a sustainable, profitable small business online? After thorough research, I will write a business plan, create my business, and focus on its development. I also plan on seeking insight from industry experts prior to starting my project. This will lead to the beginning of documentation of steps taken to create my personal graphic design business–I will list out the steps and procedures that an entrepreneur should focus on in a plain Word document before transferring to my website. My website will be created using Visual Studio Code, JavaScript Vue documentation, and Microsoft Azure web services. I will design a website that is visually pleasing and inviting–accessibility and ease of navigation will be a priority. I will also focus on providing a professional design to showcase the most important information. When writing my webpages, I will move from generic to specific with examples from my graphic design business. Once I have filled out the basic pages, I plan to add images, useful links, and videos to fully explain the procedures taken to open a small business. When the website is complete, I will summarize my process and findings into a PowerPoint presentation (specifically, this will be used for my presentation at ASPIRE). The bulk of the work will involve the set up of the website after establishing the small business. The business itself should take approximately 30 days to create and finesse. I can begin the website soon after.

The ultimate outcome of my Honors Project will have many components: a graphic design business, a website detailing the procedures associated with creating a small business, a PowerPoint presentation showing the process of and summarizing my project, and a short essay attached to my website describing what I would do differently. Although this may seem ambitious, I think it is all necessary to fully cover the project in enough detail.
